Supernova remnants

This idealized Type Ia supernova begins as an approximately spherical explosion. The simulation advances the explosion for 454 years, approximately the present age of Tycho's supernova remnant.

Check "Show only current (ℓ, m)" to see the contribution of a single spherical harmonic mode to the remnant's structure. Uncheck it to see the full superposition of all modes. Random superpositions of modes introduce large-scale asymmetry and increasingly finer structures.
